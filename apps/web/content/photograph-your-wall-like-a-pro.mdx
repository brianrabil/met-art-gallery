A flawless **PaletteMatch AR** preview starts with a sharp, distortion-free wall photo. Follow these five micro-steps and you’ll capture calibration-ready images in under a minute.

## 1. Tape the Target

- Print the **A4/Letter target sheet** from the app.
- Center it at approximate eye level; use painter’s tape to avoid residue.

## 2. Step Back Exactly 8 ft / 2.5 m

Phone lenses distort less at moderate distance, giving the AI cleaner geometry.

## 3. Square Up

Hold the phone so the sheet’s edges appear parallel to your screen—you’ll hear a *beep* once the internal gyroscope detects < 2° tilt.

## 4. Flood the Scene with Soft Light

Avoid down-lights that cast harsh shadows. Natural window light or a diffused floor lamp yields texture-true color for ΔE < 3 accuracy.

## 5. Snap in Portrait Mode (Yes, Really)

Most modern phones isolate the mid-plane in portrait mode, reducing background blur—but our algorithms keep the target razor-sharp.

> **Pro-Tip** — If your room is dim, switch on the phone’s “RAW” setting; uncompressed data improves wall-edge detection by 12 %.

### What Happens Next?

1. **Edge Detection** isolates your wall, trimming baseboards and crown molding.
2. **Depth Estimation** turns target sheet pixels into millimeters.
3. **Color Profiling** measures dominant hues using CIELAB, ensuring art and frames land within ΔE ≤ 10 of your palette.

Hit *Confirm* and watch the artwork snap into perfect scale—no measuring tape required.
